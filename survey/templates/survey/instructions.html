How the study works

You will be shown a short sequence of stimuli — they may be audio excerpts, images, or passages of text. After each one you will be asked a few brief questions about what you just saw or heard. At the end you will be asked a small number of background questions (for example age or prior training). The whole study takes a few minutes.

Please answer honestly — there are no right or wrong answers, and your responses are stored anonymously.
